Our Services

Complete Dental Solutions

From routine check-ups to advanced procedures, we offer a full spectrum of dental treatments with modern techniques.

Dental Checkup & X-Ray
Comprehensive oral examinations with digital X-rays to detect issues early and plan the best treatment.
Teeth Cleaning & Scaling
Professional scaling and polishing to remove plaque, tartar and restore a bright, healthy smile.
Root Canal Treatment
Painless RCT using advanced rotary instruments to save severely infected or damaged teeth.
Dental Implants
Permanent, natural-looking implants that restore missing teeth and improve your jaw structure.
Teeth Whitening
Professional bleaching that safely brightens your smile by several shades in one session.
Tooth Extraction
Gentle, safe extractions including wisdom teeth removal with minimal discomfort and fast recovery.
Veneers & Crowns
Porcelain veneers and crowns to restore chipped, cracked or discoloured teeth to perfection.
Dentures & Bridges
Custom-fit dentures and dental bridges for complete smile restoration and improved function.
Paediatric Dentistry
Gentle, child-friendly dental care to build healthy oral habits from a young age.
